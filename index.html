About Me

  • Last Update: Nov 18, 2022

    Patrick Lingga is an MS and Ph.D student in the Deparment of Electrical and Computer Engineering of Sungkyunkwan University since Fall 2019. His MS and Ph.D advisor is Professor Jaehoon (Paul) Jeong. He got a BS degree in July 2019 from Bandung Institute of Technology, Indonesia. His major was Telecommunication Engineering in Department of Electrical Engineering and Informatics.
